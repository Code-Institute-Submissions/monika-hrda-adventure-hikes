About

Founded in 2017, the Adventure Hikes is a group for active hikers who enjoy more challenging hikes. We organise day-long hikes in Wicklow and Dublin, weekends away all around Ireland, as well as the occasional week-long trekking trips in Europe! Check out our events section for more upcoming trips!

We have over 50 members, meeting up on the hills regularly. There has been a lot of adventure and fun had, and some beautiful friendships made. We are looking forward to meeting you!
